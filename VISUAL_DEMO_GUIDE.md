## ðŸ“Š Visual Metrics

### Animation Timing
- **Fast**: 200-300ms (micro-interactions)
- **Medium**: 500-800ms (state transitions)
- **Slow**: 2-4s (breathing, ambient)
- **Infinite**: Session lock, streaks, glows

### Color Usage
- **Emerald**: 80% (primary, progress, success)
- **Rose**: 10% (safety, attention, care)
- **Amber**: 5% (caution, consideration)
- **Stone**: 5% (neutral, secondary)

### Effect Distribution
- **Glassmorphism**: All cards
- **Gradients**: Icons, buttons, progress
- **Breathing**: Timer, badges, backgrounds
- **Shadows**: Elevation, depth, glow
