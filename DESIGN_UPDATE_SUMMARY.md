# AYURNXT HOMEPAGE DESIGN UPDATE

## Overview
Updated the Ayurnxt homepage to feature a premium Ayurvedic aesthetic with a warm ivory background, creating a trustworthy, elegant, and soothing first impression for academic and innovation judges.

---

## Color Palette

### Primary Background
- **Main Background:** `#F5F1E8` (Warm Ivory)
- **Subtle Gradient:** `#F8F5ED` (Lighter Ivory)
- Creates a clean, calm, and premium wellness aesthetic

### Accent Colors (Darker Green Tones)
- **Primary Emerald:** `#059669` (Badges, icons, accents)
- **Dark Emerald:** `#047857` (Headings, brand name)
- **Darker Emerald:** `#065f46` (Text, emphasis)

### Text Colors
- **Primary Text:** `#44403c` (Stone - warm dark gray)
- **Secondary Text:** `#78716c` (Stone Light - muted gray)
- Provides soft contrast without harshness

### Logo & Branding
- **Logo Size:** Increased to 32×32 (128px) for prominence
- **Logo Style:** Gradient from emerald-600 to emerald-800
- **Shadow:** Soft, premium shadow (0 20px 60px rgba(5, 150, 105, 0.25))

- **Size:** 6xl (60px) - larger and more prominent

- **Background:** Semi-transparent white (rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.7))

- **Gradient:** emerald-600 to emerald-800

- **Hover:** Scale 1.02 with enhanced shadow
- **Icons:** Larger, well-spaced for clarity

- **Background:** Semi-transparent white (rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.85))
